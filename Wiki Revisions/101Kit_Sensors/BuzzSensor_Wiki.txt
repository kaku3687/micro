BuzzerSensor_Description

== Description ==
The Microduino Buzzer is a passive buzzer that uses timed signals to produce different tones/sounds.

== Specifications ==
=== Dimensions ===
*Board: 20mm x 20mm
*Interface: 4-Pin, 1.27mm pitch

=== Electrical ===
*Operating Voltage: 3.3~5V
*This device takes input from a Core module to produce Output.
