InfraredReceiver_Description

== Description ==
The Microduino IR Receiver can receive infrared signals such as those used in remote control systems. Coupled with an infrared transmitter, the sensor adds remote control to any Microduino build.

== Specifications ==
=== Dimensions ===
**LED: 5mm
**Board: 10mm x 20mm
**Interface: 4-Pin, 1.27mm pitch

=== Electrical ===
**Operating Voltage: 3.3-5V
**This device produces an Output signal

=== Operational ===
**Range: 10 meters
**Frequency: 38KHz
**Receiving Angle: 45 degrees

**Pins: Signal, VCC(power), GND(ground) and NC(Not Connected).
